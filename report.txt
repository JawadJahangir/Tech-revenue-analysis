🧠 Business Insights for Management
1️⃣ Cloud Services is the strongest revenue driver

Cloud Services generated the highest total revenue (1450 million) and shows consistent growth every year.
📌 Recommendation:
The company should increase investment in cloud infrastructure and services because demand is rising rapidly.

2️⃣ AI Solutions is the fastest growing product

AI Solutions grew from 50 → 300 million in 6 years (6× growth).
This is the fastest growth among all products.

📌 Recommendation:
Expand AI product development and research, because AI is becoming a major market opportunity.

3️⃣ Software Products show stable but slower growth

Software Products revenue increased from 200 → 300 million, but the growth rate is much slower compared to Cloud and AI.

📌 Recommendation:
The company should either innovate new software features or integrate AI into existing software products.

4️⃣ Cybersecurity is steadily growing but not dominating

Cybersecurity revenue increased steadily from 90 → 250 million, indicating stable demand.

📌 Recommendation:
Cybersecurity should be positioned as a supporting product line, especially integrated with cloud services.

5️⃣ Overall company revenue growth is strong

Total company revenue grew from 460 → 1250 million in six years (almost 3× growth).

📌 Recommendation:
The company is in a strong growth phase, so expanding into new markets or scaling infrastructure is a good opportunity.

6️⃣ Future strategic opportunity: Cloud + AI integration

The two fastest growing segments are:

Cloud Services

AI Solutions

📌 Recommendation:
Develop AI-powered cloud services, which could become the company's next major competitive advantage.
